Featured Projects

A selection of my academic and extra-curricular work

Machine Learning / 3D Object Detection

DiffuBox: Refining 3D Object Detection

Developed and implemented a point-based diffusion model for refining 3D LiDAR bounding boxes.

  • Conditioned the model on LiDAR point clouds to simultaneously refine box location, scale, and rotation.
  • Addressed limitations related to domain shifts (sensor and environmental changes).
Computational Science / Statistics

Bayesian Image Analysis in Fourier Space

A Python-based Bayesian framework in Fourier space for high-accuracy restoration of microscopy images.

  • Leveraged MAP optimization and parallelized MCMC (Markov Chain Monte Carlo) sampling to manage spatial inference.
  • Achieved fast signal restoration under heavy Gaussian and Poisson noise conditions.
Python Data Analysis

Quantitative Stock Market Analysis

Quantitative historical analysis of stock market indices and financial trends in Python.

  • Processed and vectorized historical financial datasets using NumPy and Pandas.
  • Computed risk profiles, historical volatility, exponential moving averages, and optimal portfolios.
Bachelor's Thesis / Cryptography

Fondamenti matematici della Crittografia

Bachelor's thesis focused on the mathematical transition from classical cryptosystems to quantum security standards.
